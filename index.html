Wits University
Campus Transportation ▼

The Campus Navigation and Transportation App aims to enhance mobility and accessibility on campus by providing detailed interactive maps, real-time GPS tracking, and route optimization for indoor and outdoor navigation. It integrates transportation schedules and offers rental services for bikes, scooters, and skateboards, with features to locate and check availability at rental stations.

Infrastructure Management ▼

The Classroom/Venue Management App aims to streamline the process of managing classroom and venue assignments, bookings, and maintenance on campus. It features scheduling integration with academic calendars, a room booking system, and a maintenance issue reporting tool. Users can view availability through a visual calendar and receive notifications for bookings and updates.

Event & Activities ▼

The Events and Activities App aims to coordinate and manage all campus events by providing a centralized platform for event creation, management, registration, ticketing (including ticket verification), and notifications

Campus Safety App ▼

The Campus Safety App aims to enhance campus security by providing tools for real-time emergency alerts, direct incident reporting, and access to safety resources. The app features a user-friendly dashboard for receiving alerts, reporting incidents, and accessing safety information, supported by robust APIs for alert management, incident reporting, notifications, and location services. Secure and scalable infrastructure will ensure the platform can handle reasonable volumes of real-time data, enhancing the responsiveness and effectiveness of campus safety measures, thereby creating a secure environment for the entire campus community. It should integrate with the transport system to allow Campus Protection Services to respond to incidents rapidly.

Dining Services ▼

The Dining Services App aims to manage campus dining facilities and meal plans, providing a seamless and personalized dining experience. It features real-time menu access, dietary management tools, meal credit tracking, dining reservations, and a feedback system. The app includes a user-friendly dashboard for viewing menus, managing dietary preferences, and tracking meal credits, supported by robust APIs for menu data, dietary management, meal credits, reservations, and feedback

Campus Tutoring ▼

The Campus Tutoring App aims to enhance the academic support system within the university by connecting students with qualified tutors through a seamless platform. It features detailed tutor profiles, an easy booking system, virtual tutoring integration, resource sharing, and a feedback system. The app includes a user-friendly dashboard for both students and tutors, supported by robust APIs for user management, session booking, virtual tutoring, resource sharing, and feedback. Secure and scalable infrastructure will ensure the platform can handle reasonable volumes of data, providing a reliable and efficient tutoring experience for the campus community.
